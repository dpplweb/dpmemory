						<h4>History Center Cross Section</h4>
						<p>During 2016 and 2017, the <a href="http://www.desplaineshistory.org/">Des Plaines History Center</a> digitized its holdings of historic photos using grant funds from the <a href="https://cyberdriveillinois.com/departments/archives/ISHRAB/home.html">Illinois State Historical Records Advisory Board</a>. Many of these scanned images are or will be available to view on Des Plaines Memory in the History Center Cross Section and other projects.</p>
