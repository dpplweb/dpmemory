Discover, Share, Remember

Explore Des Plaines' rich history and culture with Des Plaines Memory. We have materials dating from the 1800s up to the present day. You will find pictures, postcards, letters, newspapers, diaries, pamphlets and much more. We hope that you will share, comment, and contribute your own stories about Des Plaines too!

Des Plaines Memory is a collaborative effort between the Des Plaines Public Library and the Des Plaines History Center—and you!

My Des Plaines Memory

My Des Plaines Memory collects stories about Des Plaines' past from the people who experienced it. You will find photographs, postcards, and other materials that residents have collected and contributed.

The Gallery

The Gallery on Des Plaines Memory is a great place for local artists to display their work online. Des Plaines artists can upload pictures of their artwork by filling out a simple form. Contribute now.

Local Newspapers

This collection brings together issues of several local newspapers dating from 1939 to the present. The oldest newspapers were badly damaged by water and mold and are preserved here for historical research.

This collection can be used only at the Library and the History Center

View Collection

Telephone Directories

This collection brings together telephone directories from Des Plaines and the surrounding communities. The directories are a great tool for doing genealogical and historical research.

This collection can be used only at the Library and the History Center.
