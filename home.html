Des Plaines Memory

Explore Des Plaines' rich history and culture with Des Plaines Memory. We have materials dating from the 1800s up to the present day. You will find photographs, postcards, letters, newspapers, diaries, manuscripts and much more. We hope that you will share, comment, and contribute your own stories about Des Plaines!

Des Plaines Memory is a collaborative effort between the Des Plaines Public Library and the Des Plaines History Center—and you!

History Center Cross Section

The Des Plaines History Center holds over twelve thousand photographs that document the history of Des Plaines, IL. All of these have been digitized for use in research, exhibits, and programs. This Cross Section gives a sense of the wide-ranging subjects contained in the collection. Contact the History Center to conduct research on any specific topic.

Telephone Directories

This collection brings together telephone directories from Des Plaines and the surrounding communities dating back to 1940. The directories are a great tool for doing genealogical and historical research.

This collection can be used only within the Des Plaines Public Library and the Des Plaines History Center.
